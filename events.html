Birmingham Pride Weekend — 22–25 May

Events marked below are part of Pride weekend. See our full Pride guide →

Birmingham has LGBTQ+ events most nights of the week, mostly in the Gay Village on Hurst Street.

Regular nights include drag shows at The Village Inn, karaoke at The Fox, club nights at The Nightingale, and cabaret at Eden Bar.
