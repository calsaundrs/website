🎯 The Problem We're Solving

Birmingham's LGBTQ+ community deserves better than scattered event information, outdated listings, and a disconnected scene. Finding queer events shouldn't be a challenge, and promoters shouldn't struggle to reach their audience.

For Event-Goers

  • • Hard to find up-to-date event information
  • • No easy way to discover new venues
  • • Missing out on events due to poor discoverability
  • • No mobile-friendly way to browse the scene

For Promoters

  • • Difficult to reach the right audience
  • • Time-consuming event submission processes
  • • No reliable platform to showcase their events
  • • Limited tools to manage and promote events

✨ Our Solution

We've built a modern platform that connects Birmingham's LGBTQ+ community with the events and venues they love. From our humble beginnings with a basic Squarespace site, we've evolved into a comprehensive solution that serves both event-goers and promoters.

For Event-Goers

  • • Easy event discovery with smart filtering
  • • Mobile-friendly experience that works like an app
  • • Real-time updates so you never miss out
  • • Comprehensive venue directory with detailed information

For Promoters

  • • Simple event submission process
  • • Reach the right audience easily
  • • Better tools to showcase your events
  • • Streamlined approval and management workflow
