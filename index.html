Your essential guide to LGBT events in Birmingham. From the clubs in the gay village to the groups and community keeping us loud.

Birmingham's Gay Village is an LGBTQ+ nightlife area on Hurst Street with around a dozen gay bars, clubs, and venues — a 5-minute walk from New Street station.

Use Brum Outloud to find what's on this week, browse every venue in the village, or get the full Birmingham Pride 2026 guide.
