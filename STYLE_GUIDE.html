THE 4 RULES OF TEXT
  • 1
    No Lowercase Headers

    Syne must always scream. If it's a header, `text-transform: uppercase` is mandatory.

  • 2
    Embrace The Misprint

    Use the `.misprint` CSS class on primary headers to give them that cyan/magenta ghosting effect. Don't overuse it on body text or it becomes illegible.

  • 3
    High Contrast Only

    Text must always pop. Use white, toxic lime, or electric pink on dark backgrounds. Never use dark grey text.

  • 4
    Break the Grid

    Don't be afraid to let massive typography bleed off the edge of the screen or overlap other containers using negative margins.

03. The Gob (Tone of Voice)

RULE 1

BE LOUD, BAB.

No corporate waffling. Speak like you're yelling over a techno DJ at 2 AM in a warehouse. We don't whisper; we project.

RULE 2

UNAPOLOGETIC

If a gig is going to tear the roof off, say it. Avoid polite phrases like "join us for a delightful evening." We are throwing a riot, not a tea party.

RULE 3

KEEP IT 0121

Sprinkle the local flavour. Call them "bab" or "our kid". Say the lineup is "bostin'". Keep it natural—don't go full Peaky Blinders cosplay, just a nod to our roots.
