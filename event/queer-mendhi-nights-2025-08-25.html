About This Event

Queer Mehndi Nights is especially for the LGBTQ* South Asian community, come and find a friend to practice your henna skills with, or simply just hang out for the evening. Tucked away from the bustle of the high street, we gather. The room hums with the low murmur of chatter—stories shared, laughter flowing, and a palpable sense of queer joy. This monthly ritual is where the Queer South Asian community comes together, to reconnect, create, and celebrate. For many, it’s a chance to reclaim a part of our culture and exist as our whole selves fully and unapologetically. It’s a night that feels like chosen family. Find us in the upstairs area of Loco Lounge.
