Sidewalk Reviews

Looking for reviews of Sidewalk? Here is what people are saying about this Birmingham venue.

Dóri

Very friendly stuff, grate prices. Nice enterieur. The atmosphere is very pleasant, there is a terrace which I really liked. Many cocktails to choose from, £12 for two before 8 p.m. 🏳️‍🌈👌

Nataliia B

I ordered Margarita cocktail 2 for £14, they gave me a cocktail in a half glass. It seems like someone got a big sip already from that. We went to another place on the left and the coctail service was better, at least all the glasses were full. Im pretty sure serving of cocktails is so bad there.

Matthew Lloyd AFHEA

Excellent new style and vibe. Warm and cosy. Good for chatting and hanging out. The bar now does excellent food until late too! I will defo be coming here more often now and will bring my social clubs.

Anthony Bosworth

I always come here once a month to catch up with some friends. The staff have always been welcoming and supportive to the public and they have some nice cocktail selections, shots and bombs. Usually every third week. A fur-meet take place. (To simplify what that means it’s people who love anthropomorphic animals, art and etc get to together to spread their love for the fandom). The staff have always been supportive for us and we cannot thank you enough!

Chas Crumpton

Their new food menu is amazing! So much choice for everyone! All served quickly and piping hot! Hunters chicken was beautiful, and priced amazingly!
