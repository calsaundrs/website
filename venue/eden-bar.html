Eden Bar Reviews

Looking for reviews of Eden Bar? Here is what people are saying about this Birmingham venue.

Ros

The best gay bar in Birmingham. A great vibe, friendly staff, and a well-kept, clean venue. I only discovered it recently but always enjoy my time at Eden :)

Addy

So im from St Austell and a few friends and I decided to visit Birmingham for the weekend. Now I've never been to Eden nor have my friends, but we have heard good things about Eden. It's nice and big with 2 levels and a terraces with a photo booth and a pool table all good. Now I don't drink alcohol so I stuck to soft drinks, J20, cola etc. The 2nd time I went to the bar to get a J20 i was told that ive had enough! 'Enough of what exactly' I said. The question was completely ignored and I was refused service from there on. No explanation at all. If I was drunk I could understand, but I don't drink alcohol! This member of staff was just rude and on power trip maybe. Will not be returning.

Storm

Came here for an event this weekend. Very friendly bar staff and the lady on the bar is amazing with me. Service is always fast and the venue is very roomy. Thank you for a great Saturday

Ashley Brabbin

Absolutely fantastic venue and very clean always make you feel welcome they have fantastic acts every weekend we always have a brilliant night the staff are all very friendly and a credit to the venue #bestplaceinbirminghamgayvillage

Danielle Mooney

Came here last month and the lady bouncer was an absolute babe. Spoke to us while we waited for our taxi, she deserves everything cos what a sweet woman! Drag act was amazing too! First time I’d came in years and it didn’t disappoint.
