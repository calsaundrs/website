So im from St Austell and a few friends and I decided to visit Birmingham for the weekend. Now I've never been to Eden nor have my friends, but we have heard good things about Eden. It's nice and big with 2 levels and a terraces with a photo booth and a pool table all good. Now I don't drink alcohol so I stuck to soft drinks, J20, cola etc. The 2nd time I went to the bar to get a J20 i was told that ive had enough! 'Enough of what exactly' I said. The question was completely ignored and I was refused service from there on. No explanation at all. If I was drunk I could understand, but I don't drink alcohol! This member of staff was just rude and on power trip maybe. Will not be returning.
