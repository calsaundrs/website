/// Reviews

Hannah O'Toole

A fantastic bar with a genuinely friendly atmosphere and a real sense of community. The staff are always attentive and welcoming, which makes every visit enjoyable. Prices are very reasonable, and the quality you get is great. They host excellent events, including live artists that really add to the vibe. The cocktails and coffees are top-notch, and there’s a great selection of bottled and tap drinks, plus a solid range of spirits and wines. Overall, a brilliant place to relax, socialise, and enjoy good drinks. Highly recommend!

4 months ago

Karen Eyke

Equator has been in my life for many years now. Over the years the bar has just got better and better. Service amazing, venue always clean and tidy and a warm welcome awaits you. Staff are always happy to help, nothing is too much trouble for any of them. Excellent bar - safe environment to be myself with my friends. Extremely 🏳️‍🌈 friendly.
