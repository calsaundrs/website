About The Nightingale Club

Birmingham's oldest and largest LGBT venue, The Nightingale Club is the beating heart of the local LGBTQIA+ nightlife and clubbing scene. With a massive capacity for 2000 people, this huge venue offers multiple floors of entertainment, making it the premier destination for queer nightlife in Birmingham.

From drag shows and themed nights to live performances and club nights, The Nightingale Club has been serving the LGBTQ+ community for decades, providing a safe and vibrant space for everyone to express themselves and celebrate their identity.
