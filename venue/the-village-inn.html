The Village Inn Reviews

Looking for reviews of The Village Inn? Here is what people are saying about this Birmingham venue.

Paul McCarthy

The customers here aren't out for a good night or to have fun clubbing. Most of them seem to come just looking for trouble and starting fights over the smallest things. The whole place gets way too packed which makes everything feel tense and then you’ve got people randomly throwing drinks at each other.The bar staff are proper rude and don’t seem to care at all. On top of that you’ll find drug stuff being used openly in the toilets which just makes the whole vibe feel grim and unsafe.If you want a decent night out, give this one a miss. It’s not worth the hassle or the risk of getting caught up in the aggro. Wouldn’t go back ever.
