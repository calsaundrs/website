The Fountain inn Reviews

Looking for reviews of The Fountain inn? Here is what people are saying about this Birmingham venue.

Andrew Case

I have stayed at the Fountain twice now and am heading back today for my third visit. It's my go to place to stay in Birmingham and I love it. The staff and management are lovely, friendly and helpful. The rooms are great, a good price, and very comfortable with superb bathrooms. The bar, garden, and extra facilities are great.

David Lewis

My husband and I have been staying here for about 5 years. We absolutely love the place. The staff are amazing and so lovely, the rooms are so clean (I’m a controlling clean freak btw), the music is always fabulous and the other customers are so lovely. Anything goes and everything is wonderful. I cannot highly recommend to stay or have a drink.

Richard Fletcher

Nice traditional style gay pub, mostly older customers.

Matt McSherry

A great little pub and hotel on the edge of the city centre. You can see how much the owners care for their customers. It’s a room above an old pub so there is noise and you could probably find faults if you wanted to, but for the easy going budget traveller this place is great. Wet room bathroom was a lovely bit of luxury. Bedding was excellent quality, I slept very well both nights. The owners were friendly and helpful, recommended a good local barber etc.
