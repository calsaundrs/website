OUR MISSION

Celebrating
Brum's Pride

Brum Outloud is a community-driven platform dedicated to Birmingham's vibrant LGBTQ+ scene. We believe everyone deserves to be seen and heard.

From Digbeth to the Gay Village, we track the best events, venues, and stories that make our city unique.

Values

  • ✦ Direct community impact
  • ✦ Authenticity in storytelling
