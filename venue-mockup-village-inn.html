About The Village Inn

Located at 22 Hurst Street in the heart of Birmingham's Gay Village, The Village Inn has been serving the LGBTQ+ community for years. We're more than just a pub – we're a welcoming gathering place where friends meet, drinks flow, and memories are made.

Our inclusive atmosphere combines traditional pub charm with modern entertainment to create the perfect venue for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a quiet drink, a lively night out, or somewhere to celebrate with friends, The Village Inn welcomes everyone with open arms.
